Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Central South University advised by Prof. Jiawei Huang . I am a visiting student at Singapore University of Technology and Design advised by Prof. Wenchao Jiang . I received my B.E. in Information Security from Central South University in 2016. My research interests include data center networks, Internet video, and distributed machine learning. My research work has been published in many top conferences and journals, such as ICNP, ICDCS, and ToN, TCC, WCL.
